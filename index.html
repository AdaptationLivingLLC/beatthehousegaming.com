What the App Actually Does

Roulette Breaker tracks every spin on a specific wheel and builds a complete data profile of that wheel's behavior. You need to track at least 3 consecutive series before the data becomes actionable. That's potentially 10+ hours of focused tracking at a single table before you place your first bet.
